Mission

To set up libraries in government aided schools in rural areas and help the students to improve their reading habits and knowledge.

Who we are?

We are a group of non governmental and voluntary organisations from the city of destiny - Visakhapatnam with a common aim to improve the standards of education facilities at government aided schools.

What we do?

There are library rooms in government schools but are lacking with enough number of books. So, we the voluntary organisations united to help fill the libraries in government school with required number of books.

Why we are doing?

In this 21st century, it is so sad to hear that the education system became a business for many of the private and corporate institutions who are digging the assets and properties of parents for their child to get educated. Whereas, the government schools are not too expensive and also have their standards almost equivalent to private institutions. Due to lack of awareness, the public aren't utilising the facilities provided by government aided schools.

How we do?

Set up drop-in points in and around the city.

People come, donate and enter their details in the register provided at pick up point

The books will be picked up at certain interval from the pick up points and sent to the warehouse

The books will be categorised, summed up and data will be entered in the books collection register

The books will be sent to the government schools and the libraries will be started
